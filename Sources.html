Sources Of Frustration

Reflecting on the building of this website.

Building this small website has taken me many weeks, all the little itty bitty details that you see, like the font color of the title, actually took me many hours to accomplish!

I began this website using Github and Tiny Press, but for some reason my website, no matter how hard I tried, came out looking like a website from the 90’s (pretty terrible). So, with some help, I “forked” over a template from Bootstrap called “Clean Blog” and my website transformed to a beautiful, hip and polished one. I found using Github with the Bootstrap template was much easier, the layout was quite user friendly, and it includes the basics for you, indicating where to add in your details such as photos and titles. However, easier does not necessarily mean easy, because I still managed to make some huge mistakes, one time even blanking out my entire website content - I wish there was a backspace button in Github. Slowly but surely however, I did make progress, and I am really happy with the outcome of my website as it is.

As I wrote in my “About Me” section, I chose this topic because I am fascinated with dogs. I think it is so interesting to find information and research that relates to how people and dogs interacted in the past. The information on the 1000 year old dog skeletons found in Peru, was brought to my attention in my biological anthropology class, and seeing how there was plenty of information made available through various public digital platforms, I thought it would be a good topic to explore further.
