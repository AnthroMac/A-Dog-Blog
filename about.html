About Me

A Short Summary.

Hi, my name is Mackenzie, instead of going to university straight out of high school I decided to travel. I have experienced so many beautiful places and cultures throughout my life, that when I did decide to come back to school, Anthroplogy felt like the right choice. I am now in my final year of my anthropology degree at the University of Victoria.

I chose to work on this blog project because I absolutely love dogs, I have a French Bulldog x Boston Terrier named Rose, here is a picture of her in a dinosaur costume.

I am interested in learning about dogs in antiquity, how they were utilised, domesticated or sacrificed and eventually, how they came to be one of our societies most loved pets today. There are a wide variety of examples all around the world of dogs showing up in the realm of archaeology, even today Migaloo, works as an archaeology dog, due to his keen sense of smell, Migaloo is able to sniff out bones that are hundreds of years old!

Thanks for stopping by, I hope you found this interesting.
